Publications

A sampling of my refereed AI-centric publications are below. They are divided into four categories: (1) AI-Facilitated Synchronization, Correlation, Fusion, Discernment, Diagnosis, and Forecasting, (2) AI-Facilitated Multi-Resolution Source Location Detection and Classification, (3) AI-Prioritized Network Topology, Dynamic Architectural Framework, and Decision Engineering, as well as(4) AI-Facilitated Reduction of Dark Data and Sparse Data Analysis/Actuation. Of my 72 peer-reviewed book chapters, journal articles, and conference papers (which include 33 IEEE papers), I am honored to have received 18 "Best Paper" Awards.
